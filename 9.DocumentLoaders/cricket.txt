# The Spirit of Cricket

Upon the field of emerald green,
Where countless dreams are born and seen,
A game unfolds with grace and might,
From morning sun to fading light.

The bowler marks his careful run,
His mission clear, his task begun,
With every step, the tension grows,
Until the ball through silence flows.

The batter waits with focused eyes,
Prepared for every trick and guise,
A steady stance, a fearless heart,
Ready to play a masterful part.

The leather ball begins its flight,
Swinging left and then to right,
A perfect yorker, a spinning turn,
Each delivery a lesson to learn.

The crowd erupts with every cheer,
Their voices echo far and near,
For every run and wicket won,
Adds new stories to be spun.

A cover drive, smooth as a stream,
Turns effort into living dream,
A pull shot raced across the ground,
As thunderous applause surrounds.

Then comes the six, majestic, high,
Soaring proudly through the sky,
Beyond the ropes, beyond the stands,
A moment cherished across the lands.

Fielders dive with all their will,
Climbing every challenge still,
A stunning catch, a brilliant save,
The mark of players strong and brave.

The scoreboard tells its changing tale,
Of triumphs won and plans that fail,
Yet numbers never truly show,
The passion that makes cricket glow.

In village lanes and city parks,
Beneath bright suns and evening darks,
Children dream with bat in hand,
Of representing their beloved land.

Some dream to bowl with fiery pace,
Others seek a batter's place,
Each one chasing a shining star,
Believing greatness isn't far.

Through victories that bring delight,
And defeats endured through darkest night,
The game teaches lessons wise and true,
Of patience, courage, and seeing things through.

For cricket is more than bat and ball,
More than records standing tall,
It is friendship, hope, and endless cheer,
A language understood far and near.

It unites millions, young and old,
Through stories new and legends told,
Across the world, from shore to shore,
Its magic grows forevermore.

And when the final match is done,
When every battle has been won,
The spirit of cricket still remains,
Flowing forever through our veins.

For generations yet to come,
Will hear the sound of bat and drum,
And fall in love, just as before,
With cricket's magic evermore.
